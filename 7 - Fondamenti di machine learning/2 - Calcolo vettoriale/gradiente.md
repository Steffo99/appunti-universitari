\Huge
\gradientOf{\mathbf{x}}
$$

$$
\Large
\gradientOf{\mathbf{x}}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
	\derivativePartial
		{x}
		{1}\\
	\derivativePartial
		{x}
		{2}\\
	\derivativePartial
		{x}
		{3}\\
	\vdots\\
	\derivativePartial
		{x}
		{\fmlInputSize}\\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
